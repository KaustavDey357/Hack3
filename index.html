Whats thethe deal?

HackIE³ is the third edition of our flagship student hackathon. For 36 sleepless hours, builders, designers, hardware nerds, and first-timers turn caffeine into prototypes that don't quite work yet — but might.

We don't care if you've shipped before. We care if you'll ship by Sunday.

Five tracks, twelve workshops, judges from across industry, and more snacks than your kitchen has ever seen.
